Institutional service performance reporting pages - Canada.ca template

From: Treasury Board of Canada Secretariat

Institutional service performance reporting pages collect all service standards from an institution and provide annual performance reporting information, as per the requirements of the Policy on Service.

For each of an institution’s service standards, state the level of performance that people can reasonably expect under normal circumstances, and report on past performance against those standards.

  • Working examples

When to use this template

Used once by each institution to present fiscal year service standards and performance reporting.

How to use this template

1: Page title

Indicates that the page provides annual reporting on performance against service standards for an institution

  • Content
    • Title is: [institution name] service performance reporting for fiscal year [year] to [year]
  • Presentation
    • must be a unique H1
    • must be the first element on the page

2: Institutional byline

Provides a link to the institution, organization or partnering/collaborative arrangement entity responsible for the content of the page

  • Presentation
    • use the Institutional byline pattern

3: Subsection links

Links to service standards broken into logical groupings

  • Content
    • use this componenet when there is a large number of service standards being reported on
    • group subsections logically
    • label subsections clearly and concisely
    • the text of each link must exactly match the text of the associated heading
  • Presentation
    • appears below the institutional byline
    • use the In-page table of contents pattern

4: Introduction

Provides an overview of the institution’s service standards and overall performance

  • Content
    • limit text to 1 or 2 paragraphs
    • include a summary of the institution’s service standards performance
    • written for a grade 6-8 reading level
  • Presentation
    • appears below the page title and byline

5: Subsection overview

Provides an overview of the service standards and overall performance in the current subsection

  • Content
    • limit text to 1 or 2 paragraphs
    • written for a grade 6-8 reading level
  • Presentation
    • appears at the start of each subsection of the page

6: Service standards and performance information

Describes each of the institution’s service standards and reports most recent performance results

  • Content
    • includes service standards and performance information as per the Policy on Service
    • written for a grade 6-8 reading level
    • for each service standard being reported on, provide standards, targets and performance, as follows:
      • [Service]: name of the service to which the standard applies
        • Our standard: succinct statement of the standard
        • Our target: indicate the quantifiable target
        • Our performance in [current fiscal year]: indicated the quantified performance, maximum 80 characters
  • Presentation
    • do not hyphenate words at the end of lines; retain ragged right edge for paragraphs and other text blocks
    • bullets must be used to present content

7: Past service standards and performance

Provides links to service standards and performance from previous years

  • Content
    • presents people with links to previous years’ performance results
    • heading is labeled: “Service performance reports from previous years”
    • the link to each previous year’s page of service standards is labeled: “[institution name] service performance reporting for fiscal year [year] to [year]”
  • Presentation
    • appears below the main page content
    • linked pages will apply the institutional service performance reporting page template, also ensuring a link to the current year’s institutional service performance reporting page
