Institutions list for Canada.ca: Canada.ca design

Institutions subject to “Appendix D: Mandatory Procedures for Social Media and Web Communications” of the Directive on the Management of Communications must apply the requirements of the Canada.ca Content and Information Architecture Specification.

These organizations must apply Canada.ca design requirements for all their public-facing websites or digital services, regardless of the technology, domain name (both canada.ca and gc.ca) or publishing platform through which these are delivered.
