Working with partners to improve task success on Canada.ca:

The Canada.ca Experience Office works with federal institutions to make Canada.ca content easier to find and understand.

Our method includes working collaboratively to:

  • set concrete usability targets
  • perform usability tests on specific Government of Canada Services
  • create prototypes for testing
  • refine prototypes until they achieve at least a 20% improvement in task success

Past projects with our partners

Orientation and wayfinding

Purpose: Understand how people navigate Canada.ca. The findings have led to several design changes to improve navigation throughout Government of Canada websites.

Date: 2021

Partners: Agriculture and Agri-Food Canada; Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada; Veterans Affairs Canada and Statistics Canada

Medical devices

Purpose: Make it easier for stakeholders subject to Canada’s medical device regulations to find and understand regulatory requirements.

Date: 2020

Partner: Health Canada

Electronic Procurement Solution

Purpose: Help develop a prototype for a new procurement website. We focused on the needs of businesses new to government procurement. We tested with small and medium enterprises not currently registered as suppliers to the Government of Canada.

Date: Spring/summer 2019

Partners: Public Services; Procurement Canada (PSPC)

Pay for the public service

Purpose: Make it easier for public servants to find and understand their pay information.

Date: 2019

Partner: Public Services; Procurement Canada

Partner: Canada Revenue Agency

Date: 2019

Partners: Global Affairs Canada; Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada; Public Health Agency of Canada; Health Canada; Canada Border Services Agency

Contact the CRA
