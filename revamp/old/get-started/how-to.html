What is the Canada.ca design syttem

Canada.ca is the Government of Canada digital brand for it's web presence

All institutions subject to the Directive on the Management of Communications must use the Canada.ca design system for their public-facing web presence.

  • Who has to use the Canada.ca design system

To implement that digital brand, we need a design system. We need it to:

  • build trust through a consistent experience
  • help build better, usable services faster
  • enable continuous improvement through experimentation

The Canada.ca design system is a tool to help you start with evidence-based patterns and templates, while ensuring a unified trusted digital brand for the Governnment of Canada.

Mandatory brand elements

To implement the Canada.ca brand, there are a few mandatory elements that you need to follow for every page.

These include:

  • the Canada.ca domain name
  • mandatory styles, like fonts and colours
  • a standard header and footer

Recommended templates

The design system contains severl recommended templates. These are specific arrangements of patterns aimed at helping people complete a specific taks. This includes the service initiaton page, the contact us page, the Act proile page, and many more.

These templates are battle-tested: they've been shown to help people complete their taks. They should be your starting point.

Recommended templates are flexible: you can adjust them to the specific task you're trying to help people complete.
